Hinder

CONCEPT | Branding | FIGMA - APP Design | UI/UX | Brand Touchpoints

Hinder is a daring anti-hero. It's not for everyone, this app is for risk-takers, adventurers, and yes, the hopeless romantics out there.

Like the name implies, dating isn't easy or for the faint of heart. But now we have AI for everything so why not apply it to dating too? I've designed this app to utilize the latest AI technologies as match-maker. It analyzes your answers to prompts and then finds interesting matches.
